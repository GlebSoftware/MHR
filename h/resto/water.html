About Restoration

When a water disaster occurs, it does so without warning and it must be dealt with immediately!

You need trained, reliable experts to respond quickly to mitigate damage, clean up the mess, and restore your home or business back to normal. You need IICRC trained and certified water damage restoration specialists.

MEGA Home Renovations, INC is certified in water damage restoration and we adhere to the standards of care and to the procedures taught by the Institute of Inspection, Cleaning, and Restoration (IICR).

We are available 24 hours a day and 365 days a year to tackle any water problem. When one occurs, water and sewage must be extracted, and the affected areas must be professionally cleaned, ventilated, dried, and disinfected as quickly as possible to reduce the growth of mold, bacteria, and other harmful microorganisms, which can cause additional damage to the property and potential health risks for your family or employees.

Water damage may be cause from various sources such as:

  • Flood Waters
  • Water from other units (multi-housing, apartment complexes)
  • Dishwasher or washing machine overflow
  • Fire sprinkler
  • Clogged toilets
  • Leaky roof, windows, doors, sidings, etc.
  • AC unit condensation
  • Groundwater seepage/foundation cracks
  • Busted or leaking hose or pipes
  • Severe storm or sewer backup, etc.

Every water damage situation is a little different, and requires a unique solution, but the general process stays the same. MEGA Home Renovations, INC will extract the water and pre-clean the impacted areas, followed by several restoration procedures in order to dry the structure by sanitizing and deodorizing the affected areas, installation of water damage equipment (air movers, air scrubbers, and dehumidifiers) and they will be left for a few days on the premises, depending on how extensive the damage is. We will come back to monitor the equipment, temperature, humidity and moisture. The steps listed below illustrate our process for the “typical” water damage emergency.

  1. Emergency Contact (770-342-8338; 24/7)
  2. Inspection and Damage Assessment
  3. Water Removal/Water Extraction
  4. Drying and Dehumidification
  5. Cleaning and Sanitizing
  6. Restoration

Call MEGA Home Renovations, INC. 24/7 and 365 days a year for professional water damage restoration you can trust and to prevent any further water damage.
