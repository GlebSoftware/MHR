Mold Remediation

When water intrudes into your home or apartment, mold growth can start very quickly- in as little as 48 hours. Consider the following mold facts:

  • Mold spores thrive on moisture.
  • Mold spores can quickly grow into colonies when exposed to water.
  • These colonies may produce allergens and irritants and have the potential to cause other health effects.
  • Mold spores are microscopic, float along in the air, and may enter your property through windows, doors, or AC/heating systems or even hitch a ride indoors on your clothing or a pet.
  • Mold is present almost everywhere, indoors and outdoors.
  • Mold often produces a strong, musty odor, and that odor can lead you to possible mold problem areas.
  • Even higher-than-normal indoor humidity can support mold growth.
  • Before mold remediation can begin, any sources of water or moisture must be addressed. Otherwise, the mold may return!

If you see or smell any signs of mold call MEGA Home Renovations, Inc. Our mold specialist will meet with you to devise a plan for mold testing and remediation.

Every mold damage scenario is different and requires a unique solution, but the general mold remediation process stays the same.

  2. Inspection and Mold Damage Assessment
  3. Mold Containment
  4. Air Filtration
  5. Removing Mold and Mold-Infested Materials
  6. Cleaning Contents and Belongings
  7. Restoration

Often, additional testing is needed. Additional testing means the technician needs to collect air and surface samples to send to a special laboratory testing facility. The lab will provide a report of what kind of mold is/are present, and how much. Following the testing and inspection we will submit a written mold remediation report and scope of work to properly eliminate the mold.

During the mold remediation process, we will utilize HEPA filtration machines to clean the air; cut affected areas and sand/scrub/wire brushing.

Our specialists will remove the affected materials such as drywall, trims, baseboards and place them in plastic bags for disposal. We then will proceed to perform thorough sanding, wire brushing, and anti-microbial treatment. This stage also includes duct cleaning and installation of dehumidifiers.

During the final inspection stage, we will do a post-moisture test and remove the dehumidifiers. Depending on our initial agreement, we either issue our final report and invoice or proceed to the reconstruction phase to restore your property back to normal.
