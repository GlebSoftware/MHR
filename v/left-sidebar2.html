Origin 629

Unit 629 is a wonderful representation of Origin resorts. Located right across the street from the best beach in the continental USA, Origin Resorts features amenities that’ll truly make you pop, from an in-resort movie theater, to the world-famous Sharky’s Restaurant right across the street, staying at 629 will be an experience you’ll never forget.

The unit features a luxurious, “studio”-type feel, with 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom, and 5 sleeping spots. We have a mini-kitchen if you desire to cook, but the internationally-renowned Pier Park is a meager mile away, featuring mouth-watering food, thriller attractions, and incredible entertainment. Our unit has 2 flat-screen TVs, each with Netflix and Disney+, for a relaxing stay in or a fun night out.
