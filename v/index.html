About Splash

Enjoy the newest Sterling resort located on over 400 feet of pristine gulf front, located on the west end of Panama City Beach! Splash is located on the West end of Panama City Beach, convenient to Pier Park- Panama City Beach's new premier center for dining, entertainment, and shopping. Enjoy a wide variety of restaurants there, such as Jimmy Buffet's Margaritaville, Guy Harvey's, Back Porch Seafood, and Reggae J's. See a movie at 16 Grand Theatre (which has stadium seating), take a ride on the carousel, or other Miracle Strip rides. Indulge in shopping at Dillards, JCPenney, Ron Jon Surf Shop, or any of the other retail & specialty shops at Pier Park.

Located on over 400 feet of a sugar-white beach, Splash has amenities unequaled in Panama City Beach: Two distinct, family-fun waterpark environments with waterslides, dive-in movies in the summer, lazy river, water cannons, water toys, hot tub, and outdoor pool heated seasonally. Health club, game room with mini bowling alley, beachside activity lawn, poolside bar & grill open seasonally.

We include a free beach service for all of our splash rentals! This includes 2 cushioned wooden lounge chairs and an umbrella reserved for you right on the beach for your ENTIRE stay! Just walk down a few stairs to the beach and everything is ready and waiting for you. This is an additional $40 per day bonus value that we have prepaid for you!!!!



About Origin

Origin at Seahaven Resort is located right across the Gulf of Mexico. It has easy access to the beautiful white beaches of PCB. The building has 4 elevators, covered parking, a fitness room, poolside grilling, an arcade, an on-site laundry room, a huge heated zero edge swimming pool, a hot tub overlooking the Gulf, and an in-house movie theater. It is minutes away from Pier Park - 900,000 square feet of a family entertainment complex with shopping, dining, and entertainment. The building is just a short walking distance from the dolphins at Gulf World, and a short walk to many other activities- like golf courses, putt-putt, jet ski rentals, fishing, boating, sailing, and lots of other great vacation activities!
